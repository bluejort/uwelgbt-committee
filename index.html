About Us

Welcome to UWE's LGBT+ Society. We're the home of UWE's LGBT+ student community, organising regular socials, friendly activities, and nights out in Bristol. Why not join our Discord server to find out what's going on and how to get involved?

How can I get involved?

We're currently looking for new committee members - you can see the list of vacancies at the bottom of the page! To talk about this, or to ask about or request a charity or event please feel free to message a member of the committee using the provided details.

We are passionate about fundraising, so if that's something that speaks to you please don't hesitate to get involved. In 2019 we won RAG's Fundrasing society of the year award after raising and donating £3,564 to amazing LGBT+ charities, and while Covid-19 has put a damper on our efforts, we're absolutely raring to raise even more in the coming year.

Who can become a member?

Anyone! So long as you are a part of UWE, you can join us and get involved. (We're all about inclusivity and equality here!)
